 2 - Warning ! It can be loud !
 
  Groovit is devolute to make sounds. But please, always keep in mind that very loud sound may be generated with it, that's why I recomend to get use to it with small and costless devices first before trying it on a HiFi or stage amplifiers.
Normally with standards sound card you should not produce more signal than the D/A converter can produce, but you can generate very low and powerfull waves that may damage you speakers if you're using a powerfull bass system.
