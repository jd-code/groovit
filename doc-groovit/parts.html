Groovit's global parts.
  Groovit consist in three main modules : the biggest one on the first top half the screen is the drum matrix. The next one at the bottom of the screen : the analog voices. The third one, the dynamic filter is just above.
There is also an important part, right in the middle of the screen, this part is used to edit various values : it's the levels editor.
